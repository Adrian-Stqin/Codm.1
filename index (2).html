Call of Duty: Mobile is a free-to-play first-person shooter game developed by TiMi Studios and published by Activision. It was released on October 1, 2019, for iOS and Android devices. The game brings together elements from various Call of Duty titles, offering a mix of classic maps, modes, and characters from the franchise.

1. Multiplayer Modes: Includes popular modes such as Team Deathmatch, Domination, and Search and Destroy.

2. Battle Royale: Features a 100-player battle royale mode with solo, duo, and squad options.

3. Zombies Mode: Offers cooperative gameplay against waves of zombies, although this mode has been periodically added and removed.

4. Customization: Players can customize their loadouts with various weapons, attachments, perks, and scorestreaks.

5. Seasons and Events: Regular updates and events that introduce new content, such as maps, weapons, and characters.

6. Competitive Play: Includes ranked modes and seasonal leaderboards for competitive gameplay.

The game has been praised for its graphics, controls, and faithful recreation of the Call of Duty experience on mobile devices.
